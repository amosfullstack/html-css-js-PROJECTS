Welcome to the University of Dodoma

Welcome to the University of Dodoma (UDOM), a vibrant hub of academic excellence and innovation located in the heart of Tanzania. We are delighted to have you as part of our dynamic community, where students, faculty, and staff collaborate to push the boundaries of knowledge and contribute to the development of our society.


At UDOM, we offer a diverse range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs designed to equip you with the skills and knowledge necessary to thrive in an ever-evolving global landscape. Our state-of-the-art facilities, cutting-edge research, and dedicated faculty members provide a supportive environment for your academic and personal growth.


We pride ourselves on fostering a culture of inclusivity, creativity, and critical thinking. Whether you are pursuing studies in science, technology, engineering, arts, humanities, or social sciences, you will find ample opportunities to engage, explore, and excel. Join us in our mission to create a better future through education, research, and community engagement. Together, we will continue to make significant strides in addressing the challenges and seizing the opportunities of our time.

Welcome to the University of Dodoma where your journey to success begins!


Prof. Amos K. Ragner Vice Chancellor, University of Dodoma


About Our University

The University of Dodoma (UDOM) is situated approximately 8 km east of the city center of Dodoma, Tanzania's capital. Accessible by readily available public transport, UDOM's strategic central location makes it an ideal choice for students across the country. This position is particularly beneficial for government and private sector employees in Dodoma, who can now easily pursue further education and training without compromising their work commitments. UDOM's geographical setting and favorable weather also make it an attractive option for international students seeking a conducive learning environment. With a commitment to academic excellence and innovation, UDOM provides a supportive atmosphere for both local and international students to advance their careers and personal growth.
